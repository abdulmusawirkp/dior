The Lady 95.22 bag

(Re)birth of an icon

Revealed at the Dior autumn-winter 2022-2023 ready-to-wear show, the Lady 95.22 bag revisits the House's unique heritage with modernity. An ode to the link between past and present, it reinvents the lines of the Lady Dior and echoes the year 1995, the date of the icon's creation, and 2022, the year of this original reinterpretation. Its ultra-contemporary, subtly curved silhouette makes it a fashion statement, a promise of a bold and sophisticated look. The two handles, delicately refined with leather and metal, are punctuated with "Dior" charms, evoking the lucky-charms of Monsieur Dior.
